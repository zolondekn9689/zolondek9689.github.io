Why did I pick University of Wisconsin Stout?

I chose Stout because of their hands on program. Since, Software Development is a hands on major, I believed that the program should be hands on. UW Stout was a college that has what I wanted. The second reason I picked Stout was the co-op requirement they require of their students. Having a co-op would help jump start my career. The third reason I picked UW-stout was the concentrations inside each major. All the other schools, I had looked at did not have concentrations and focuses like UW-Stout has.
